Buttered Noodles

Buttered noodles are simple to make with your favorite pasta, butter, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper for a quick and easy, kid-friendly dish. Fresh herbs and a little lemon juice could be added to amp up the flavor. Perfect to serve either as-is or alongside steak, chicken, or meatballs.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package fettuccine noodles
  • 6 tablespoons butter, cut into pieces
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
  2. Stir in fettuccine, bring back to a boil, and cook pasta over medium heat until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes.
  3. Drain and return pasta to pot. Mix butter, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper into pasta until evenly combined.
